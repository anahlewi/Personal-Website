About Me

I am a rising junior at Wellesley College. Since I was a little girl whether I was in school solving math problems or hanging out with my friends on the weekend, surfing the web was an essential part of my daily routine. As my passion for technology grew, I sought out programs that would help me expand my knowledge, this is what led me to find the Girls Who Code Summer Immersion Program, where I spent seven weeks at the AT&T Office in NYC and learned how code. After completing the program I went on to intern at AOL for the Cambio website. As of now I currently serve on the Executive Board for the Wellesley College Computer Science Club. Additionally, I am one of the Social Media Coordinators/Wordsmiths for WHACK (Wellesley College's Hackathon).

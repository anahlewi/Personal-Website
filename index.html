About

I am a First Year student at Wellesley College. Since I was a little girl, whether I was in school solving math problems or hanging out with friends on the weekend, surfing the web was an essential part of my routine. As my passion for technology grew, I sought out programs that would help me expand my knowledge, which led me to the Girls Who Code Summer Immersion Program where I learned how to code. Afterwards I went on to intern at AOL where I was responsible for updating cambio.com. Ever since, I have been recognized for my accomplishments in the tech industry. Most recently I was recognized as one of Huffington Post’s 20 Under 20: STEAM Pioneers.

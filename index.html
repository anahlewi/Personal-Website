About

Anah Lewi , a native of Brooklyn, a rising senior, and currently an Intern at Cambio, has always been passionate about technology. Since she was a little girl whether she was in school solving math problems or hanging out with friends on the weekend, surfing the web was an essential part of her daily routine. As her passion for technology grew, she sought out programs that would help her expand her knowledge, this led her to the Girls Who Code Summer Immersion Program, where she learned how to code and loved it. Aside from being a girl who codes, Anah is an active blogger, social activist, youtuber and loyal Beyonce stan. She hopes to someday successfully launch her own tech company and become a full-time member of the Beygency.
